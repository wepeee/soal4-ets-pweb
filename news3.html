Haiti's Gang Violence Crisis: UN Arms Embargo

October 22, 2024

The United Nations Security Council has expanded an arms embargo to address the escalating gang violence in Haiti. Armed groups have taken control of much of the capital, Port-au-Prince, leading to clashes with local police and increasing the need for humanitarian aid. This move is aimed at preventing gangs from gaining further access to illicit weapons and worsening the conflict.
